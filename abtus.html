OUR VISION

A premier global manpower solutions provider and a trusted partner in providing quality workers and professionals.

OUR MISSION

1. To achieve a world-class standards in the aspect of overseas manpower and human resources recruitment with focus on customer satisfaction.
2. To provide assistance to qualified Filipino workers by ensuring them competitive compensation and benefits from their foreign employers.
3. To provide skills development trainings to qualified Filipino workers in order to make them competitive globally.
4. To observe and practice excellent customer service.
5. To have full compliance with legal and regulatory requirements.
6. To practice and observe high degree of professionalism in dealing with overseas clients.
7. To constantly adapt innovations.

COMPANY'S COMMITMENT

To its PRINCIPALS and FILIPINO WORKERS:

PMDL commits to be an active partner of and assist the Government in providing overseas employment for Filipino workers

PMDL commits to provide its PRINCIPALS with supply of highly qualified and excellent workers, and be a trusted partner in their human resources needs.

PMDL commits to provide Filipino workers with meaningful careers in foreign countries.

ABOUT THE COMPANY

PMDL INTERNATIONAL MANPOWER SERVICES ("PMDL") is a sole proprietorship
Company duly registered with the Department of Trade and Industry (DTI) under
Business Registration Certificate No. 03142331.

PMDL is duly licensed by the Philippine Overseas Employment Administration
(POEA) and the Department of Labor & Employment (DOLE) to engage in
international land-based manpower recruitment business.

PMDL was established and organized by its Proprietor, Atty. Pedro M. de Leon
Jr., to pursue his passion and advocacy of making a difference in the lives of
others, by providing employment opportunities to millions of Filipino workers;
mindful of the reality that employment is a key to poverty alleviation.

PMDL, aside from manpower recruitment, also offers skills trainings and
personal/career development programs to qualified Filipino workers to make
them highly competitive in the global labor supply market.

OUR SERVICES

PMDL provides manpower sourcing services for various organizations, companies and business establishments across the globe, particularly in Japan, Malaysia, Middle East, Saipan (Northern Marianas Island), Equatorial Guinea, Africa, as well as in other parts of the Asia Pacific Region.

We offer recruitment and placement services for companies seeking to find qualified and competent professionals and workers from the Philippines. We provide solutions to the manpower needs of companies in the industrial/technical, oil and gas, power & energy, construction, manufacturing and agricultural industries.

As we strictly adhere to excellent customer service, we "walk the extra mile" in providing personal services to our foreign principals and recruited workers.

Our focus is on sourcing and providing the talents and services that deliver efficiency and innovation.

We provide full life cycle manpower recruitment support.

PMDL ensures that your search for an ideal candidate requires just a single phone call. We usually find you a qualified candidate within 24 HOURS of your notice/call.

What does PMDL do to customers?

I. SCREENING OF APPLICANTS

PMDL has a data bank of applicants which is methodically checked, verified and screened. We pre-screen all candidates to identify their skills and verify their qualifications.

II. INTERVIEWING

Equipped interview rooms are available for Principal's representatives. Applicants are pre-screened before presentation to Principal's representatives.

III. MEDICAL EXAMINATION

PMDL engages professional services of a modern industrial clinic/medical center to conduct medical examination to Filipinos intending to work abroad.

IV. DOCUMENTATION AND PASSPORTING

We handle all necessary documentation and assist with passport processing for selected candidates.

V. PRE-DEPARTURE ORIENTATION SEMINAR

Comprehensive orientation on work culture, expectations, and living conditions abroad to prepare workers.

VI. COUNSELLING AND LIAISING WITH FAMILIES

We provide ongoing support and communication channels for workers' families back home.

VII. TRAININGS AND MANPOWER DEVELOPMENT

We offer skills development trainings to qualified Filipino workers to make them highly competitive in the global labor market.
