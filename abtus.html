COMPANY HISTORY

PMDL is duly licensed by the Philippine Overseas Employment Administration (POEA) and the Department of Labor & Employment (DOLE) to engage in international land-based manpower recruitment business.

PMDL was established and organized by its Proprietor, Atty. Pedro M. de Leon Jr., to pursue his passion and advocacy of making a difference in the lives of others, by providing employment opportunities to millions of Filipino workers; mindful of the reality that employment is a key to poverty alleviation.

PMDL, aside from manpower recruitment, also offers skills trainings and personal/career development programs to qualified Filipino workers to make them highly competitive in the global labor supply market.

PMDL is a service-oriented entity, a bridge for Filipinos to prove their skills and expertise to the world.

COMPANY'S COMMITMENT

To its PRINCIPALS and FILIPINO WORKERS:

PMDL commits to be an active partner of and assist the Government in providing overseas employment for Filipino workers

PMDL commits to provide its PRINCIPALS with supply of highly qualified and excellent workers, and be a trusted partner in their human resources needs.

PMDL commits to provide Filipino workers with meaningful careers in foreign countries.

OUR VISION

A premier global manpower solutions provider and a trusted partner in providing quality workers and professionals.

OUR MISSION

1. To achieve a world-class standards in the aspect of overseas manpower and human resources recruitment with focus on customer satisfaction.

4. To observe and practice excellent customer service.
